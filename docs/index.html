What is Quant AI?

Quant AI is an AI investment research assistant for equities, crypto assets, and market themes. It supports conversational research, evidence comparison, watchlist review, portfolio questions, technical and fundamental context, quantitative signal interpretation, and AI quant trading research.

Quant AI is built for research and strategy analysis. It is not a broker, not an automated trading bot, not an order router, and not a personalized financial advisor.
